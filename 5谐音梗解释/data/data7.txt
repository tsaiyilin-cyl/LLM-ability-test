Cn:
患者听到医生说："Mercury is in Uranus right now"，患者不解："我又不信天象的"，医生说："我也不信天象啊，我意思是体温计裂开了"。
En:
The patient heard the doctor say: "Mercury is in Uranus right now." Puzzled, the patient replied: "I don't believe in astrology " The doctor said: "I don't believe in astrology either—I mean the thermometer broke."
梗在于：Mercury有"水星"、"水银"两个意思，Uranus有天王星的意思，所以患者理解水星在天王星正位，实则医生的意思是"Mercury is in your anus"：水银掉在你的肛门里了（体温计裂开）
